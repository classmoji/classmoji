❓ Pricing FAQ

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate your billing accordingly.

What happens if I exceed my student limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can either upgrade your plan or remove inactive students to stay within your limit.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for educational institutions. Contact us to learn more about our academic programs.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial of Pro features. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and can accommodate institutional purchase orders for annual subscriptions.
