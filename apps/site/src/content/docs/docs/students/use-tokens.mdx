### What tokens are

Tokens are how you buy extra time on a late assignment, no email or awkward ask required. Your instructor gives you a balance of tokens, and you spend them to extend a deadline by however many hours you need.

### Spending tokens

When an assignment is past its deadline and you haven't submitted, it shows a **Late** tag on your **Assignments** page with an **Extend** control in the actions column. Open it and:

1. Pick how many hours you want, up to the hours you're currently late
2. Check the cost. It's the tokens-per-hour rate for that assignment times the hours you chose
3. Confirm

The tokens come out of your balance and those late hours are bought back, so they no longer count against you. If you don't have enough tokens for the hours you picked, the purchase is blocked and you'll see how many you're short.

### Checking your balance and history

Open the **Tokens** page in your classroom to see your current balance and a full log of your transactions: tokens you were given and tokens you've spent, with dates. Nothing is hidden, so you can always see where your tokens went.
