title: Join a classroom

---

### Before you start

Your instructor adds you to a classroom using your school email. You don't sign up for a class yourself, you just create your account and the invite is waiting for you.

### Step 1: Sign in with Github

Go to [app.classmoji.io](https://app.classmoji.io) and click **Continue with Github**. Classmoji uses Github for sign-in, so there's no separate password to remember.

### Step 2: Verify your school email

Enter your school email and Classmoji sends you a 6-digit code. Type the code back in to verify it. The code is only valid for a few minutes, so check your inbox right away.

Then fill in your name and school ID. Your Github username is filled in for you.

### Step 3: Land in your classroom

If your instructor already added you, the invite is matched to your school email (or the email on your Github account) automatically and you go straight to your classroom. If you signed up before being added, your classroom shows up as soon as your instructor adds you.

### Accepting the Github invite

The first time you open your classroom, Github sends you an invite to join the course's Github organization. Accept it. You need to be in the organization to get access to your assignment repositories. Until you accept, your membership shows as **Pending**. Once you're in, it becomes **Active** and your repos appear.
